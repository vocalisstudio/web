## What You Get for Free

First, let's be clear—the free version includes many features.

| Feature | Free |
|---------|------|
| Scale Playback | ✅ All 9 types |
| Real-time Pitch Detection | ✅ |
| Basic Recording | ✅ |
| Key Progression Settings | ✅ |
| Preset Saving | ✅ |

**Limitations**:
- 5 recordings per day
- 30 seconds per recording

For daily warm-ups and short practice sessions, the free version works well.

## Features Added with Premium

### Recording Limits Removed

| Item | Free | Premium |
|------|------|---------|
| Daily Recording Limit | 5 | Unlimited |
| Recording Duration | 30 sec | 5 min |

Practice with longer scale patterns and extensive key progressions without restrictions.

### Pitch Graph

View your pitch trajectory as a graph after recording.

![Pitch Graph](/images/en/04_pitch_graph_fullscreen.png)

- Visualize pitch changes over time
- Compare against target scale notes
- Identify exactly where you went off-pitch

Learn more: "[Understanding the Pitch Graph](/en/posts/pitch-graph-guide/)"

### Spectrogram Display

Visualize frequency components over time.

- See the harmonic structure of your voice
- Check vocalization stability
- Useful for voice quality analysis

Learn more: "[Spectrogram Basics](/en/posts/spectrogram-basics/)"

### Detailed Statistics

View your recording analysis as concrete numbers.

![Statistics](/images/en/05_statistics_pitch.png)

**Pitch Analysis**:
- Overall accuracy (%)
- Median cents deviation
- Sharp/flat rate

**Pitch Details**:
- Note onset accuracy
- Pitch stability score
- Interval accuracy

**Vibrato Analysis**:
- Average rate (Hz)
- Average extent (cents)
- Regularity (%)

Learn more: "[Understanding Statistics](/en/posts/statistics-guide/)"

### Unlimited Recording Storage

Save as many recordings as you want locally. Compare past recordings to see your progress.

## Who Is Premium For?

### Serious Practitioners

If 5 recordings per day isn't enough, or you want to practice with longer scales.

### Analytical Learners

If you want to understand your voice through numbers and graphs, not just feelings.

### Progress Trackers

If you want to compare past recordings and see tangible improvement.

## Try Free First

Start with the free version to experience the app's basic features.

- How scale playback feels
- Real-time pitch display
- General usability

If you find it useful, consider upgrading to Premium.
