## Blog Launch Announcement

We have launched the Vocalis Studio development blog.

On this blog, we plan to share the following content:

- **Update Information** - Announcements about new features and improvements
- **User Guides** - Effective ways to use the app
- **Music Production Tips** - Voice training and recording techniques
- **Behind the Scenes** - Technical initiatives and future plans

## About Vocalis Studio

Vocalis Studio is a voice recording and analysis app for iOS.

Key features:

- High-quality voice recording
- Real-time waveform display
- Pitch analysis and visualization
- Recording data management
