Understand your voice
through data.

Record along with scale playback and visualize pitch in real-time. A simple practice tool for voice training.

iOS 15.0+ Multi-language StoreKit 2

Features

Recording with Scale

Auto-play scales and record your voice. Countdown leads seamlessly into practice.

Real-time Analysis

Spectrum and pitch indicator visualize volume and pitch changes instantly. Cent deviation display supported.

Flexible Settings

Adjust scale type, starting pitch, tempo, progression pattern, and ascending/descending cycles.

SCREENSHOTS

Practice flow, reflected in UI.

Recording → List → Analysis — intuitive navigation throughout.

Recording List

Auto-organized with date, key, and scale info. Select to play or analyze instantly.

Recording Analysis

Spectrogram and pitch graph show the difference between target scale and detected pitch.
