Vocalis Studio Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 6, 2024

Important Notice: Vocalis Studio prioritizes user privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information with third parties.

1. Basic Policy

The provider of Vocalis Studio (hereinafter referred to as "the App") (hereinafter referred to as "We") respects user privacy and strives to protect personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App obtains and how it is used.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit the following information:

  • Personal identification information such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Photos or videos
  • Device identification information (including advertising identifiers)
  • Internet browsing history
  • Any other personally identifiable information

2.2 Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on the user's device and is never transmitted externally:

  • Recording data (audio files)
  • Recording date and time information
  • Pitch analysis results
  • App settings (language settings, volume settings, etc.)
  • Recording count (for managing free plan limitations)

2.3 Information Managed by Apple

Information related to subscription purchases is managed by Apple Inc. We do not have access to the following information:

  • Credit card information
  • Personal information associated with Apple ID
  • Detailed purchase history

We can only verify whether a user has purchased the Premium Plan.

3. Purpose of Information Use

Information stored locally is used only for the following purposes:

  • Playback and management of recording data
  • Display of pitch analysis results
  • App functionality improvements and user experience enhancement
  • Management of free plan usage limitations
  • Maintaining user-configured settings such as language and volume

4. Information Sharing

The App does not share user information with third parties.

All data is stored on the user's device and is never transmitted over the internet.

5. Data Retention Period

Recording data and analysis results are stored on the device until deleted by the user.

Users can delete data in the following ways:

  • Delete individually from the recording list within the app
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app

6. Security

The App implements the following measures to protect user data:

  • All data is stored on the device and not sent to external servers
  • iOS sandbox functionality restricts access from other apps
  • Access to recording data is limited to within the app only

7. Microphone Access

The App requests permission to access the device's microphone to provide recording functionality.

Microphone use is limited to when the user presses the record button.

Recording data is stored only on the user's device and is never transmitted externally.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Since the App does not collect personal information, it can be used safely regardless of age.

9. Links to Other Services

The App may contain links to external websites (such as Terms of Use and Privacy Policy).

These external sites are not managed by Us and have their own privacy policies.

10. About Apple App Store

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store.

Information regarding downloads, updates, and purchases on the App Store is managed according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

11. Crash Reports

Currently, the App does not collect automatic crash reports.

If crash reporting functionality is introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated and users will be notified.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy as necessary.

When changes occur, they will be posted on this page and notified within the app.

If there are significant changes, consent may be requested when updating the app.

13. User Rights

Since the App does not collect personal information, the following rights do not need to be exercised:

  • Right to access personal information
  • Right to correct personal information
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to data portability

All data stored on the device, including recording data, is fully controlled by the user and can be deleted at any time.

14. International Data Transfer

The App is fundamentally designed for offline operation and does not transfer data internationally.

All data is stored on the user's device.

15. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the following methods:

  • Inquiry form within the app
  • App support page on the App Store

16. Applicable Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Japan.

Summary

Vocalis Studio Privacy Protection Highlights:

  • ✅ We do not collect any personal information
  • ✅ All data is stored only on your device
  • ✅ No data transmission over the internet
  • ✅ No data sharing with third parties
  • ✅ Users can delete data at any time
